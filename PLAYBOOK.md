# Money Day — Hour-by-Hour Playbook

**Goal:** $50-200 in cash today. Zero budget. Cold start.
**Strategy:** One sharp productized offer, posted to 4 buyer channels, harvest replies.

---

## The Offer (Primary)

**"$99 Landing Page in 24 Hours — built, deployed, fully yours"**

- 1-page site, mobile-responsive, custom design (not Wix junk)
- Hosted on your Vercel free tier OR handed off as a GitHub repo
- Includes contact form, real copy, AI-generated hero image
- 1 round of revisions
- Delivered in under 24 hours from payment

**Why this offer:**
- Web dev is real in your stack (Vercel + design skills)
- $99 is impulse-buy territory for small biz / solopreneurs / coaches
- "Today" is the unique angle — every other freelancer takes a week
- One sale = goal hit

## The Offer (Backup, for volume)

**"$39 Resume/CV redesign — modern, ATS-friendly, delivered in 4 hours"**

- Universal demand, no buyer risk, fast turnaround
- Use ChatGPT for copy polish + your design eye for layout
- Post on r/forhire and Upwork in parallel

## Hour-by-Hour Timeline

### Hour 0-1 — Set up the storefront
- [ ] Pick a name: e.g. "shipfast.day" / "todaysite.dev" / "onedaylanding.com"
  - Or just use your existing GitHub Pages / Vercel subdomain — no domain needed
- [ ] Deploy `offer.html` (in this folder) to Vercel: `vercel --prod` from this dir
- [ ] Set up Stripe Payment Link (free, 5 min): https://dashboard.stripe.com/payment-links
  - Or PayPal.me link as fallback
- [ ] Drop payment link into the landing page CTA

### Hour 1-2 — Build 2 portfolio examples
- [ ] Pick 2 fake-but-plausible niches (e.g. "Dog walker in Brooklyn", "Pilates studio")
- [ ] Generate hero image via ComfyUI / Higgsfield
- [ ] Build each as a real deployed landing page (you can ship 2 in 1 hour with shadcn)
- [ ] Screenshot them, add to your offer page gallery

### Hour 2-3 — Post everywhere
Post the 4 prepared messages (see `posts/` folder):
- [ ] **r/forhire** — Make a [FOR HIRE] post (Sundays/weekdays both work)
- [ ] **r/slavelabour** (lower price tier — also post the $39 CV offer there)
- [ ] **Upwork** — 10 personalized proposals to "landing page" / "one page website" jobs
- [ ] **LinkedIn** — 1 post on your feed, 10 DMs to small business owners

### Hour 3-8 — Active grind window
- [ ] Refresh r/forhire HIRING tab every 20 min, reply to fresh posts within 5 min
- [ ] Monitor Upwork "Available now" jobs, send proposals fast
- [ ] Reply to every comment/DM within 10 min — speed is your conversion edge
- [ ] When someone shows interest: send Stripe link IMMEDIATELY, don't "schedule a call"

### Hour 8-24 — Deliver
- [ ] As soon as you have $99 in hand: 4 hours to ship the landing page
- [ ] Use shadcn + Vercel + a generated hero image
- [ ] Over-deliver: 2 hero options, deployed to their domain if possible, repo handed off

## Conversion-killers (avoid these)

- **"Let's hop on a quick call"** — kills momentum. Sell async, deliver async.
- **Custom quotes** — your price is $99 flat. Custom = "I have to think about it."
- **Long discovery questions** — ask 3 max: business name, what they sell, who buys.
- **Portfolio gating** — show work immediately, don't make them click "view my work."
