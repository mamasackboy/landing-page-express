# Upwork — proposal template

**Strategy:** Send 10 proposals today. Target jobs posted in the last 6 hours, $50-500 budget, "landing page" / "one page" / "simple site" in the title. Skip enterprise jobs and "looking for long-term partner."

## How to find good jobs

Search filters:
- Project length: Less than 1 month
- Job type: Fixed price
- Budget: $50-500
- Posted: Last 24 hours
- Keywords: "landing page", "one page website", "simple website", "small business site"

## Proposal template (personalize the first line every time)

---

Hi [Client first name],

I noticed you need a landing page for your [their business type — e.g. "yoga studio", "cleaning service", "SaaS"]. I can have a live URL in your hands inside 24 hours for **$99 flat**.

**What I'd build:**
- Custom design (not a template) — hand-coded React + Tailwind
- Mobile-first, fast, accessible
- AI-generated hero image matched to your brand
- Working contact form sending to your email
- Deployed to your domain or a free subdomain
- Full GitHub repo handed to you on delivery

**My process is intentionally minimal:** you accept this proposal, answer 3 quick questions (business name, what you sell, who buys it), and within 24 hours I send you a live URL plus 1 revision round.

You can see two examples of work I've shipped in this format here: [YOUR-URL-HERE]

If you want something with more pages or features, happy to quote separately — but for a single landing page, I'm confident this is the fastest, cleanest path to "live site" you'll find on Upwork.

Ready to start the moment you accept.

— [Your name]

---

## Notes

- **Personalize line 1.** Read their job post. Name their business type or a detail from the post. Generic copy-paste = instant skip.
- **Lead with price.** Most freelancers hide it. Stating $99 upfront filters out tire-kickers and signals confidence.
- **No questions in the proposal.** Don't ask "can you tell me more?" — they wrote the job post for that. Answer their need directly.
- **Connects cost:** Each proposal is 4-6 Connects ($1-1.50). 10 proposals = $10-15. You have 0 budget, so cap to your existing free Connects balance (~10 free/month for new accounts).
- **Boost a single high-value proposal** if you have Connects to spare — places you at top of client's list.

## After they reply

If they say "yes" or ask for next steps, send IMMEDIATELY:
> "Great. Pay via Upwork (escrow keeps you safe). Once you fund the milestone, send me: 1) business name, 2) what you sell in 1 sentence, 3) who your customer is. I'll have a live URL to you within 24 hours."

Do NOT offer a call. Do NOT pad with pleasantries. Speed kills competitor proposals.
