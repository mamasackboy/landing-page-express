# LinkedIn — feed post + cold DMs

## Strategy

LinkedIn has the highest-paying buyer pool (small business owners, consultants, coaches, agency owners). The funnel:
1. **One feed post** announcing the offer (warm-touches your existing network)
2. **10 cold DMs** to small business owners with bad/no website

Send DMs in 2 waves: 5 in morning, 5 in afternoon. LinkedIn throttles aggressive senders.

---

## Feed post

> I'm running an experiment this week: **$99 for a fully-built, deployed landing page in 24 hours.**
>
> Custom design. Mobile-first. AI-generated hero image. Live URL + full GitHub repo in your hands the next day.
>
> Most freelancers take a week and charge $1k+. I'm fast and I work alone.
>
> Taking 2 orders today. DM "site" if you want one.
>
> [YOUR-URL-HERE]

**Posting tips:**
- Post between 8-10am or 5-7pm your timezone (LinkedIn algorithm rewards engagement spikes)
- No hashtags in the post body — LinkedIn's algo now penalizes them
- If you have ANY connections in your network, tag 1-2 small business owners with "this might help you"
- Include 1 screenshot of a portfolio piece — posts with images get 2x reach

---

## Cold DM template

**How to find targets (5 min on LinkedIn):**
1. Search: `"founder" OR "owner"` + your city + filter "1st + 2nd connections"
2. Look for: small businesses (5-50 employees), service businesses (gyms, salons, consultants, agencies), bio mentions website that looks bad or has no link
3. Skip: anyone with a marketing director in their team, anyone at a tech company, anyone with a polished personal site

**The DM (under 300 chars — LinkedIn's free message limit):**

---

Hey [First name] — saw your work at [Company]. Looked at your site and noticed a few quick wins. I'm doing a $99 / 24-hour landing page experiment this week — full custom build, deployed, source code yours. If a fresh page would help, happy to send a sample. No call needed.

---

**Why this works:**
- "Saw your work at [Company]" = proves you looked
- "Few quick wins" = curiosity hook without insulting their current site
- "$99 / 24 hours" = price + speed upfront, no bait-and-switch
- "No call needed" = removes the #1 friction point
- 60-second read, 5-second reply

## Follow-up if no response (3 days later, NOT today)

> Just bumping this — I have a slot open Wednesday if you want to grab it. URL: [link]

That's it. Don't 3-touch on day 1, that's spammy and burns the contact.

---

## What NOT to do on LinkedIn today

- Don't connect-and-pitch — LinkedIn detects and shadow-bans
- Don't mass-DM with the exact same text — vary 3 lines per message
- Don't use "growth hack" tools (Dripify etc.) — instant ban risk
- Don't pitch in connection request notes — they get auto-filtered
