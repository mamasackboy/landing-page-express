# Client intake + delivery scripts

These are the messages you send AFTER someone says "I want it." Copy-paste, fill placeholders, send.

---

## Message 1 — "Yes I want it" → payment

Send within 5 minutes of their reply. Speed = conversion.

> Awesome. Here's the Stripe link — payment is fully refundable if I don't deliver: [STRIPE-PAYMENT-LINK]
>
> The moment that clears, reply to this message with:
>
> 1. Your business name
> 2. What you sell, in 1 sentence
> 3. Who buys it (your ideal customer)
>
> Optional bonus info that speeds things up:
> - A color or vibe you want (e.g. "warm, professional" or "playful and bold")
> - 1-2 sites you like the look of
> - Your domain if you want me to deploy there (else I'll use a free subdomain)
>
> You'll have a live URL within 24 hours from when I get the intake. Most deliveries land in 4-6 hours.

---

## Message 2 — Delivery email

Send when the site is live. Include screenshots inline.

> [Their first name] — your site is live: **[URL]**
>
> Quick rundown of what's there:
> - **Hero:** [headline you wrote]
> - **3 sections:** [section 1], [section 2], [section 3]
> - **CTA:** routes to [their contact form / phone / Calendly / etc.]
> - **Contact form:** submissions go to [their email]
>
> **Source code:** [GitHub repo link, set to private, invite their account]
>
> **One revision round is included.** Look it over, send me anything you want changed in one message (not piecemeal — it helps me batch). 24h turnaround on revisions.
>
> If it's good as-is, a public review on [Upwork/Fiverr/Reddit/wherever they found you] is the best thank-you. Future clients live and die by reviews.
>
> Enjoy the new site. Reply anytime if questions come up later.

---

## Message 3 — Revision request → done

>
> Changes:
> - [change 1]
> - [change 2]
> - [change 3]
>
> Latest commit on the repo if you want to see the diff.
>
> If you ever need a multi-page version, blog, e-commerce, etc. — just ping me, I'll quote separately. Otherwise, ship it.

---

## Message 4 — Upsell (send 3 days after delivery)

> Hey [name] — checking in. How's the site holding up? Any submissions through the contact form yet?
>
> If you want to expand it (more pages, blog, booking system, A/B test on the hero), I'm running another batch this week starting at $79 per extra page.
>
> If everything's smooth, ignore this and enjoy. Just keeping in touch.

This single follow-up converts at ~15-25% if the original delivery was good. Free money.

---

## Refund handling

If someone asks for a refund:
1. Don't argue. Issue it immediately via Stripe dashboard (1 click).
2. Reply: *"Refunded — should hit your card in 5-10 days. Sorry it didn't land. If you want to tell me what missed, I'd appreciate the feedback."*
3. Move on. Refund rate at this price point is <5%. Don't let one bad one tilt you.
