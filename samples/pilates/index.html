Our approach

The opposite of a fitness factory.

01

Small groups, always

Six reformers, six students, two instructors. You will be seen, corrected, and called by name in every class.

02

Form over flash

We don't do trendy hybrids or 75-cue power flows. Classical reformer work, paced so your body actually learns it.

03

Built for sticky knees

Most of our regulars came in with an old running injury, a desk-job back, or a postpartum core. We meet bodies where they are.
